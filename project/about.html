History

Halo Catering Services began in 2015 as a small family-run kitchen, founded by Terri-Ann Bassaragh, a passionate home cook known for her flavorful Caribbean dishes. What started as preparing meals for friends and church events quickly grew into a full-fledged business through word-of-mouth and community support. In 2017, the company expanded into event catering, serving weddings, birthday parties, and corporate functions across the city. With a strong commitment to fresh ingredients, personalized service, and cultural authenticity, Flavors & Festivities became a trusted name in local catering. By 2020, the business opened its first commercial kitchen and hired a professional team, allowing them to offer custom menus, buffet services, and meal delivery for both large and small events. Today, Halo Catering Sevices continues to grow, proudly serving diverse clients and bringing people together through great food.
